Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

FlappyBird ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the FlappyBird mobile application ("App").

Summary: FlappyBird does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App operates entirely offline on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

FlappyBird is a fully offline, single-player arcade game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, advertising ID, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Contact information or address book data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

2. Local Data Storage

The App stores game progress data locally on your device. This includes:

  • Level completion status and star ratings
  • High scores and best scores per level
  • Game settings preferences

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party. If you delete the App, all local data will be permanently removed.

3. Third-Party Services

FlappyBird does not integrate with any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Advertising SDKs or networks
  • Analytics or tracking tools
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage or sync services

4. Children's Privacy

FlappyBird is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under any age. Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is fully protected when using our App.

5. No Internet Connection Required

FlappyBird is designed to work completely offline. No internet connection is required to play the game. The App does not make any network requests during normal operation.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated policy posted within the App or on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
